To add the database, please follow these steps:
1. Click the gear icon on the top right;
2. Choose "Admin settings" in the drop-down panel;
3. Click "Add a database" in the page;
4. Fill all fields according to db_info.json into this form, concretely:
Database type: PostgreSQL
Display name: PostgresTomDB
Host: localhost
Port: 5432
Database name: TomDB
Username: Tom
Password: Tom123456
5. Click the "Save" button on the bottom.
