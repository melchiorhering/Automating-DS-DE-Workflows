To complete the visualization, please follow these steps:

1. Click the "New" button on the top right;
2. Choose "Question" in the drop-down panel;
3. Choose "Orders" in the "Sample Database" drop-down panel;
4. Click the "Join data" button under the "Data" panel;
5. Click the "Pick Data... -> Products";
6. Choose Summarize "Count of rows" by "Product -> Category";
7. Click "Sort -> Count";
8. Click on the arrow beside the "Count" to change the sorting order as descendingly;
9. Click on Visualize.
10. Click on Visualization -> Funnel.
11. Click Done.
12. Click the "Download full results" button (cloud icon) on the bottom right;
13. Choose ".png" in the panel;
14. Open the local path "/home/user/Downloads";
15. Rename the visualization file as "funnel_chart.png".
