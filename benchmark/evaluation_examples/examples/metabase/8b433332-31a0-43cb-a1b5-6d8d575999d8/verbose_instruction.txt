To complete the visualization, please follow these steps:

1. Click the "New" button on the top right;
2. Choose "Question" in the drop-down panel;
3. Choose "Products" in the "Sample Database" drop-down panel;
4. Under "Summarize", click "Pick the metric you want to see";
5. Choose "Sum of ..."
6. Then Click "Price"
7. After "by", Click "Pick a column to group by"
8. Click "Category";
9. Click on the plus sign after the "Category" and choose "Created At - Quarter";
10. Click on Visualize;
11. Click on Visualization -> Bar;
12. Click on Bar again to pull out the Bar Options panel;
13. Under Display - Stacking, choose Stack;
14. Click Done;
15. Click the "Download full results" button (cloud icon) on the bottom right;
16. Choose ".png" in the panel;
17. Open the local path "/home/user/Downloads";
18. Rename the visualization file as "stack_chart.png".
