To ask the question, please follow these steps:
1. Click the "New" button on the top right;
2. Choose "Question" in the drop-down panel;
3. Choose "Orders" in the "Sample Database" drop-down panel;
4. Click "Pick the metric you want to see";
5. Choose "Average of ..." in the panel;
6. Choose "Total" in the panel;
7. Click "Pick a column to group by";
8. Choose "User ID" in the panel;
9. Click "Sort";
10. Choose "Average of Total" in the panel;
11. Click the "+" icon on the right of "Average of Total";
12. Choose "User ID" in the panel;
13. Click "Row limit";
14. Input "10" in the input box.
