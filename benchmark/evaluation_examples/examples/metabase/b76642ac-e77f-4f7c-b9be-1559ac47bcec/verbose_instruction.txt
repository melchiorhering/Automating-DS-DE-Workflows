To create the Metabase question, please follow these steps:
1. Click the "New" button on the top right;
2. Choose "Question" in the drop-down panel;
3. Choose "Orders" in the "Sample Database" drop-down panel;
4. Click "Pick the metric you want to see";
5. Choose "Custom Expression" in the panel;
6. Type "Share([Tax] > 5)" in the EXPRESSION input box;
7. Type "tax percentage" in the NAME input box;
8. Click the "Done" button;
9. Click "Pick a column to group by";
10. Choose "User ID" in the panel.
