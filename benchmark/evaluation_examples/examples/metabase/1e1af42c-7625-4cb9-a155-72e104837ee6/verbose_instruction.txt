To complete the visualization, please follow these steps:

1. Click the "New" button on the top right;
2. Choose "Question" in the drop-down panel;
3. Choose "Orders" in the "Sample Database" drop-down panel;
4. Click on Visualize;
5. Click on the "Summarize" button at the top right corner.
6. Under "Group by" choose "Total" - Add grouping;
7. Try each option in "Auto binned" drop-down menu until find one which makes the y-axis ranging around 1,400;
8. Click Done;
9. Click on Visualization -> Bar;
10. Click on Bar again to pull out the Bar Options panel;
11. Under Display, open "Show values on data points";
12. Choose "Values to show - All";
13. Choose "Auto formatting - Compact";
14. Click Done;
15. Click the "Download full results" button (cloud icon) on the bottom right;
16. Choose ".png" in the panel;
17. Open the local path "/home/user/Downloads";
18. Rename the visualization file as "histogram.png".
