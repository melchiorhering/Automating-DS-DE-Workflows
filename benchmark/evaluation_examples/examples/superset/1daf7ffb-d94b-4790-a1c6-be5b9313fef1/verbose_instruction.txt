To create a pivot table 'TravelPrivot' for the dataset 'flight' to see the sum of cost in each mouth from 2011-1-1:00:00:00 to 2011-6-30:00:00:00 and keep 3 level of column: department, travel class and mouth, we can follow these steps:
1. In the opened web page, select "Charts" in the top menu;
2. Click '+CHART' in the top right corner;
3. In the pop-up window, click "Choose a dataset" box;
4. Select "flights";
5. In right box, click "Pivot Table";
6. Click "CREATE NEW CHART" in the bottom;
7. In the new window, drag the "Travel Class","department" and "Travel Date" title from left list to the "COLUMNS" box ;
8. Click "TIME GRAIN" box;
9. Select "Month";
10. Drag the "Cost" title from left list to the "METRICS" box ;
11. Click "SAVE" in the small pop-up window;
12. Click "Travel Date(No filter)" in the FILTERS box;
13. Click "TIME RANGE" box in the small pop-up window;
14. Click "RANGE TYPE" box;
15. Select "custum";
16. Click "START (INCLUSIVE)" box;
17. select "Specific Date/Time";
18. Select time "2011-01-01:00:00:00" in the left time box and click "Ok";
19. Select time "2011-06-30:00:00:00" in the right time box and click "Ok";
20. Click "APPLY";
21. Click "CREATE CHART" in the bottom;
22. Click "SAVE" in the top right corner;
23. Type "TravelPrivot" in "CHART NAME" box;
24. Click "SAVE", and you can see table "TravelPrivot" in the main panel.
