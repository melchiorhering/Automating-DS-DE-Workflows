To show the action log of user "Superset Admin" in ascending order of time, we can follow these steps:
1. In the opened web page, click "Settings" in the top right corner;
2. Select "Action Logs";
3. In the new window, click "FELTER LIST";
4. Select "USER";
5. Click "Select Value" box;
6. Select "Superset Admin";
7. Click "Search",
8. Click "dttm" in the list and  you will see the action log of user "Superset Admin" in ascending order of logical date.
