In this task, we want to sort the hardware list by 3 fields:
1. Click the funnel icon on the top menu bar (which is the middle icon under page title "servicenow"). The filter will show up after loading.
2. Click the "Add Sort" button in the row under the funnel icon.
3. Find the selection bar under prompt "Order results by the following fields", click it to choose option.
4. Type in "Display name" and select the filtered entry.
5. Since we want to sort the results in descending order, we click and select the option "z to a" in the box next to "Display name".
6. Next, we click the button "Add Sort" again to add another condition.
7. In the second field box, we click and type in "Cost center". Choose this entry.
9. About the sort method, we click and choose "a to z", that is acending order.
10. Lastly, we add the 3rd sorting method via clicking button "Add Sort".
11. Click and type in "Stockroom" into the third sorting field. Enter the filtered entry.
12. Click and select the sorting method to "a to z".
13. Click the "Run" button and wait for page reloading.
