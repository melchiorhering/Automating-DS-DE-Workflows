We want to order 10 "Development Laptop (PC)" in the hardware store. To do this:
1. Click the "Hardware" panel and wait for page loading.
2. In the search bar on the top-right (with text "Search catalog"), click it and type in "Development Laptop (PC)".
3. Press Enter to filter the items. We will see the desired item panel. Click it.
4. Let's fill the detailed configuration. For "What size solid state drive do you want?", click the radio box starting with value "250".
5. For "Please specify an operating system", select "Ubuntu".
6. Come to the right panel, click and choose the "Quantity" with value "10".
7. Click the button "Order Now". Wait a few seconds, the request has been made.
