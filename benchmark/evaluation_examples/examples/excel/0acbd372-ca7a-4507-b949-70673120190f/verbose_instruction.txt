The instruction is about net income calculation and data formatting
1. In the opened NetIncome.xlsx, click C1
2. In the input field under tool bar, type in "Net Income"
3. Click enter to confirm the change
4. Click C2
5. In the input field under tool bar, type in "=$A2-$B2"
6. Click enter to confirm the change
7. Click the bottom right corner and drag until you reach C20
8. Click on A1 and drag until all data is covered in orange to select all the data
9. Right click, and choose "Format Cells" at the bottom of pop-up bar
10. In 'Category' list at the left of window, find and click 'Currency'
11. Click 'OK' at bottom right of window to confirm format setting
12. Save the file
