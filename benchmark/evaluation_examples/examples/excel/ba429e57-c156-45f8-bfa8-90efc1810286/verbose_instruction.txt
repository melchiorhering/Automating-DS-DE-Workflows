To calculate the due date in this excel:
1. Click cell C1;
2. Type in text "Maturity Date" and press Enter;
3. Click cell C2;
4. Type in "=A2+B2" and press ENTER;
5. Click the bottom right corner of cell C2 (you should see a cross symbol);
6. Hold the mouse and drag it to cell C10, then release the mouse;
7. Save the result via Ctrl+S.
