This task requires you to create a pivot table and a line chart.
1. Select all columns from column A to G.
2. Click "Insert", and choose "Pivot Table".
3. Click "OK".
4. Drag "Week" into "Raw Fields", and drag "Revenue ($)" into "Data Fields".
5. Click "OK".
6. Click "Insert", and choose "Chart".
7. Choose "Line chart", and click "Finish".
8. Save the sheet using Excel 2007-365 format.
