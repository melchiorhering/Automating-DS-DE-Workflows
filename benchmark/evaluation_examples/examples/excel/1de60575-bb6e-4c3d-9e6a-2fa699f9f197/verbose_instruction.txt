Summarize the total revenue for each promotion type in a new sheet (Sheet2) with the promotion names as the column headers. We can follow these steps:
1. Press Ctrl+A to select all data.
2. Click button "Insert -> Pivot Table..." in the top menu bar.
3. Click OK to confirm the data selection.
4. Press and drag `Promotion` from `Available Fields` to `Column Fields` (top middle box).
5. Press and drag `Revenue` from `Available Fields` to `Data Fields` (bottom middle box).
6. Click button OK to create the pivot table.
7. Right click the new sheet `Pivot Table_Sheet1_1`.
8. Choose "Rename Sheet..." in the pop-up sub-menu.
9. Type text "Sheet2" in the `Name` field.
10. Click button `OK` to apply the change.
11. Press Ctrl+S hotkey to save the file.
