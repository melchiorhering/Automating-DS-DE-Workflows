This task requires you to tune the AdaBoost classifier to achieve a 1.0 accuracy. Follow the steps:
1. In the "Building the AdaBoost Model" section, change the ``n_estimators`` of AdaBoostClassifier from 1 to 40.
2. Change the ``learning_rate`` of AdaBoostClassifier from 0.1 to 0.8.
3. Click the button "Run" in the menu bar on the top.
4. In the drop-down menu, select and click "Run All Cells".
5. Wait until all cells completes running, then click the button "File" in the menu bar on the top.
6. In the drop-down menu, select and click "Save All".
