This task aims to create a job to constantly check source freshness.
1. Click "Deploy" in the upper panel.
2. Choose "Jobs".
3. Click "Create job", and choose "Deploy job".
4. Now you will see the configuration page of a new job. In "Job settings" section, set the "Job name" to "check_source".
5. In "Execution settings" section, check "Run source freshness" tick box.
6. In "Triggers" section, enable "Run on schedule".
7. Click "Timing" selection list, and choose "Interval" as the timing scheme.
8. Click "Run every (UTC)" section, and choose "Every 1 hour" in the drop-down panel.
9. Click "Save" button on the top right of the page.
