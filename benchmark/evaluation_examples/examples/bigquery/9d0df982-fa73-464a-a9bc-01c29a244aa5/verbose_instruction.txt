BigQuery provides many wonderful open datasets for free access. Today, we will explore one related to baseball. To achieve this goal:
1. In the Explorer pane, click +Add.
2. In the **Add** dialog, search `public datasets`
3. Then click "Public Datasets".
4. In the search bar next to header "Marketplace", type in "baseball" and click Enter.
5. There should be only one dataset called "MLB 2016 Pitch-by-Pitch". Just click it.
6. After loading, click the button "VIEW DATASET".
7. Switch to the new opened web tab. It exhibits the dataset info about baseball. This is exactly what we want.
