This task requires you to connect dbt cloud to an Bigquery project.
1. In the "Choose a connection" section, choose "BigQuery".
2. Click the "Next" button.
3. Now the "Configure your environment" section will expand. Click the "Upload a Service Account JSON file" button under "Settings" subtitle.
4. Navigate to "Desktop" in the pop-up browser window.
5. Double-click the file "dbt-user-creds.json" and upload it.
6. Click the "Test connection" button to confirm whether the connection is successfully established.
7. If the connection succeeds, click the "Next" button to save the changes.
