We will add 3 sorting methods for the Catalog Items.
1. Click the funnel icon on the top menu bar (which is the middle icon under page title "servicenow"). The filter will show up after loading.
2. Click the "Add Sort" button in the row under the funnel icon.
3. Find the selection bar under prompt "Order results by the following fields", click it and type "Created by". Confirm our choice.
4. Choose the sorting method "z to a".
5. Click the "Add Sort" button again.
6. In the new selection bar, click and type in "Flow". Confirm it.
7. For sorting option, choose "z to a".
8. Click the "Add Sort" button again.
9. In the new selection bar, click and type in "Classic Mobile Picture Type". Confirm it.
10. For sorting option, choose "z to a".
11. Finally, click the "Run" button and wait for the results.
