To order 5 "Apple MacBook Pro 15\"" in the hardware store, we need to:
1. Click the "Hardware" panel and wait for page loading.
2. In the search bar on the top-right (with text "Search catalog"), click it and type in "Apple MacBook Pro 15\"".
3. Press Enter to filter the items. We will see the desired item panel. Click it.
4. Come to the right panel, click and choose the "Quantity" with value "5".
5. Click the button "Order Now". Wait a few seconds, the request has been made.
