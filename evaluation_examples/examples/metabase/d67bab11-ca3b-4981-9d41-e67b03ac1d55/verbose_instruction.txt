To finish this task, please follow these steps:

1. Click the "New" button on the top right;
2. Choose "Model" in the drop-down panel;
3. Click on the "Use a native query" card.
4. Copy the SQL query in 'model_query.sql' file and paste it in input panel.
5. Click the "Run query" button on the right side, and check the output data.
6. Click on "Save" button on the top right corner;
7. Type in "model1" under "name", and click "Save".
8. Click on the button to the right bottom corner "Download full results".
9. Click on ".json".
10. Switch to "/home/user/Downloads" folder, and rename the json file as "model.json".
11. Copy and Paste it to the Desktop.
