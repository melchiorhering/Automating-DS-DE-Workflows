Today's task is to create two tables in a new sheet showing the total revenue for each product and sales channel. To finish it,
1. Click the "Append sheet" button at the bottom of the sheet panel (on the left of `Sheet1` tab) to create a new sheet called "Sheet2".
2. Switch back to Sheet1 via clicking the Sheet1 tab.
3. Select columns A to G.
4. Click the menu button "Insert -> Pivot Table...".
5. In the pop-up window, click OK to confirm the selected area.
6. Press and drag the `Product` entry from `Available Fields` box to `Row Fields` box.
7. Press and drag the `Revenue ($)` entry from `Available Fields` box to `Data Fields` box.
8. Click "Source and Destination" row to select the output position.
9. Under `Destination` split, click the radio button `Selection`.
10. In the input box coming after `Selection`, type in the position "$Sheet2.$A$1".
11. Click button "OK" to close the pop-up window.
12. Next, in the Sheet1, select columns from A to G again.
13. Click the menu button "Insert -> Pivot Table...".
14. In the pop-up window, click OK to confirm the selected area.
15. Press and drag the `Sales Channel` entry from `Available Fields` box to `Row Fields` box.
16. Press and drag the `Revenue ($)` entry from `Available Fields` box to `Data Fields` box.
17. Under `Destination` split, click the radio button `Selection`.
18. In the input box coming after `Selection`, type in the position "$Sheet2.$D$1".
19. Click button "OK" to close the pop-up window.
20. Press Ctrl+S to save the excel file.
