To finish the `Total` row in the table and insert the chart, we do as follow:

1. CLick on the cell $A$12.
2. Type "Total", and press Enter.
3. Click on the cell $B$12.
4. Click on the input line on top of the Excel table.
5. Type "=SUM()", and select the cells $B$2:$B$11. Press `Enter`.
6. Move the cursor to the right-bottom corner of cell $B$12 to see the cursor turns into a cross. Then drag the cursor from $B$12 to $G$12. Now we complete the `Total` row of the table.
7. Start making the chart. Press `Ctrl` and select the cells $A$12:$G$12, $B$1:$G$1.
8. Click on the botton `Insert` from the top of the page.
9. In the drop down selector, select `Chart...`.
10. Click on `Line` under "Choose a chart type".
11. Click on the graph `Lines Only` at the right side.
12. Click `Next`.
13. Click `Next`.
14. Click on the `Title` input box.
15. Type "Total".
16. Uncheck the check box before "Display Legend".
17. Click `Finish`.
