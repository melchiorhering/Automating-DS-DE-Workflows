To create the group and add the member, please follow these steps:
1. Click the gear icon on the top right;
2. Choose "Admin settings" in the drop-down panel;
3. Click the "People" tab on the top bar;
4. Click the "Groups" tab on the left side;
5. Click the "Create a group" button on the top right;
6. Type "Project Users" in the input box;
7. Click the "Add" button on the right side;
8. Click the "Project Users" to enter the group page;
9. Click the "Add members" button on the top right;
10. Type "J" in the input box and select "John Wilson" from the drop-down list;
11. Click the "Add" button on the right side.
