To reset and update the password, please follow these steps:
1. Click the gear icon on the top right;
2. Choose "Admin settings" in the drop-down panel;
3. Click the "People" tab on the top bar;
4. Click the three dots (...) on the right of the John Wilson account;
5. Choose "Reset password" in the drop-down panel;
6. Click the "Reset password" button;
7. Click the icon on the right side of the "Show" button to copy the temporary password;
8. Click the "Done" button;
9. Click the "Exit admin" button on the top right;
10. Click the gear icon on the top right;
11. Choose "Account settings" in the drop-down panel;
12. Click the "Password" tab in the center of the page;
13. Paste the temporary password in the "Current password" input box;
14. Type "metabase123" in the "Create a password" input box;
15. Type "metabase123" in the "Confirm your password" input box;
16. Click the "Save" button.
