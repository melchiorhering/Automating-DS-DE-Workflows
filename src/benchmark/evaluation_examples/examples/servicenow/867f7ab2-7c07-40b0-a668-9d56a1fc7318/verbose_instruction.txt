We want to sort the incidents list by Caller in ascending order:
1. Click the funnel icon on the top menu bar (which is the middle icon under page title "servicenow"). The filter will show up after loading.
2. Click the "Add Sort" button in the row under the funnel icon.
3. Find the selection bar under prompt "Order results by the following fields", click it to choose option. Type in "Caller" and choose this option.
4. For the sorting method, click and choose "a to z".
5. Finally, click the button "Run" and waiting for the execution to finish.
