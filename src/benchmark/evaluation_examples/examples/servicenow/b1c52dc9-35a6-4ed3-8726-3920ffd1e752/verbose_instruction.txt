This task asks us to sort the assets results according to residual value (descending) and display name (ascending). To finish it:
1. Click the funnel icon on the top menu bar (which is the middle icon under page title "servicenow"). The filter will show up after loading.
2. Click the "Add Sort" button in the row under the funnel icon.
3. Find the selection bar under prompt "Order results by the following fields", click it to choose option.
4. Type in "Residual value" and select the filtered entry.
5. Since we want to sort the results in descending order, we click and select the option "z to a" in the box next to "Residual value".
6. Next, we click the button "Add Sort" again to add another condition.
7. In the second field box, we click and type in "Display name".
8. Press Enter to confirm this choice.
9. About the sort method, we click and choose "a to z", that is acending order.
10. Finally, we click the button "Run" under the funnel icon. And wait for the engine to complete it.
