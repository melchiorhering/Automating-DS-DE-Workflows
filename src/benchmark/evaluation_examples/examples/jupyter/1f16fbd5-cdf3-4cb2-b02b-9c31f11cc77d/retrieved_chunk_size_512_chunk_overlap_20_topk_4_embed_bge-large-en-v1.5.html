Jupyter notebooks (.ipynb files) are fully supported in JupyterLab.The notebook document formatused in JupyterLab is the same as in the classic Jupyter Notebook. Your existing notebooks should open correctly in JupyterLab. If they don’t, please open an issue on our GitHub issuespage.
