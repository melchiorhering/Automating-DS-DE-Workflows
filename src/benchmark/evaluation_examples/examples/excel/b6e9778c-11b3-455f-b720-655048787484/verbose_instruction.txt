This task requires you to create a summary table and a pie chart.
1. Select all columns from column A to G.
2. Click "Insert", and choose "Pivot Table".
3. Click "OK".
4. Drag "Product" into "Raw Fields", and drag "Revenue" into "Data Fields".
5. Click "OK".
6. Click "Insert", and choose "Chart".
7. Choose "Pie chart", and click "Finish".
8. Save the sheet using Excel 2007-365 format.
