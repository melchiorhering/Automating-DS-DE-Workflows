To achive this goal, we need to do as the following steps:

1. Press `Ctrl` and select cells $A2:$F$36.
2. Click on the button on top of the page `Data`.
3. In the drop down panel, select `Sort...`.
4. Click on the drop down panel under "Sort key 1".
5. Choose "Column A".
6. CLick "OK".
7. Start making the chart. Press `Ctrl` and select the cells $A$1:$A$36, $E$1:$E$36.
8. Click on the botton `Insert` from the top of the page.
9. In the drop down selector, select `Chart...`.
10. Click on `Line` under "Choose a chart type".
11. Click on the graph `Lines Only` at the right side.
12. Click `Next`.
13. Click `Next`.
14. Click on the `Title` input box.
15. Type "Quantity".
16. Uncheck the check box before "Display Legend".
17. Click `Finish`.
