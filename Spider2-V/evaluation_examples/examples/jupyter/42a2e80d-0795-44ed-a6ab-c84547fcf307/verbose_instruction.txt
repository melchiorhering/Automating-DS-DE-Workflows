You need to completed notebook_1, add the contents of notebook_2 on the desktop to the back of notebook_1 and run them all. In detail,
1. In the left panel, double-click notebook_2, there are 6 cells need to be copied.
2. Press the hotkey "Ctrl+A" to select all cells and "C" to copy.
3. Click "notebook_1.ipynb" on the top to switch to the tab.
4. Select the last cell of this notebook.
5. Press "Esc" to enter command mode.
6. Press "V" to paste.
7. Click "Run > Run All Cells" at the top of the tab.
8. Press "Ctrl+S" to save the file.
