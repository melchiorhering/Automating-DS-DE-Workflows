This task requires you to create an environment for an existing project.
1. Click the big green "Create Environment" button at bottom.
2. Click the "Environment name" text bar, change the value to "dev env".
3. Click the "Environment type" bar, choose "Development" in the drop-down panel.
4. Click the "dbt version" bar, choose "1.5" in the drop-down panel.
5. Click "Save" button on the top right of the page.
