To create the API key, please follow these steps:
1. Click the gear icon on the top right;
2. Choose "Admin settings" in the drop-down panel;
3. Click the "Authentication" tab on the left menu;
4. Click the "Set up" button under the "API Keys" section;
5. Click the "Create API Key" button in the center;
6. Type "main" in the "Key name" input box;
7. Choose "Administrators" in the "Select a group" drop-down box;
8. Click the "Create" button on the bottom right of the panel.
