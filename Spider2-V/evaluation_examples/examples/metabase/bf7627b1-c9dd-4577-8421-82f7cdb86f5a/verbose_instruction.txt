To delete the metabase sample database, please follow these steps:

1. Click the gear icon on the top right;
2. Choose "Admin settings" in the drop-down panel;
3. Click "Databases" button in the top of the page;
4. Click "Sample Database".
5. Click "Remove this database".
6. Type "Sample Database" in the box that appears on the screen.
7. Click the "Delete" button on the bottom.
