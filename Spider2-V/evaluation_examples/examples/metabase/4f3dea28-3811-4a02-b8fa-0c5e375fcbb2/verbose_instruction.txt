To complete the visualization, please follow these steps:
1. Click the "New" button on the top right;
2. Choose "SQL query" in the drop-down panel;
3. Copy the SQL query from the opened "query.sql" file and paste it into the blank worksheet;
4. Click the "Run query" button on the right side;
5. Click the "Visualization" button on the bottom left;
6. Choose the "Bar" visualization type;
7. Click the gear icon on the top right of the "Bar" button;
8. Select PRODUCT_ID for the X-axis;
9. Select AVG(TAX) for the Y-axis;
10. Click the "Download full results" button (cloud icon) on the bottom right;
11. Choose ".png" in the panel;
12. Open the local path "/home/user/Downloads";
13. Rename the visualization file as "visualization.png".
