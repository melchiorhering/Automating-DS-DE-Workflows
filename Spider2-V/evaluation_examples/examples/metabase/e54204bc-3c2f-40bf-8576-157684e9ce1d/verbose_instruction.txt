To complete the visualization, please follow these steps:

1. Click the "New" button on the top right;
2. Choose "Question" in the drop-down panel;
3. Choose Raw Data > Sample Database, then pick the Orders table. 
4. Click Visualize.
5. Then click the Visualization button at the bottom to bring up the Visualization sidebar.
6. Choose the "Line" visualization type;
7. Click on the Summarize button on the top right corner.
8. Under Summarize by, delete "Count" and add a metric of "Sum of Total".
9. Under Group by, choose "Created At - by month".
10. Click "Done".
11. Click the Visualization button at the bottom to bring up the Visualization sidebar.
12. Click "Line" to bring up the Line options.
13. Click on Display.
14. Click on Goal line, and type 40000 under Goal value.
15. Click on Trend line.
16. Click Done.
17. Click the "Download full results" button (cloud icon) on the bottom right;
18. Choose ".png" in the panel;
19. Open the local path "/home/user/Downloads";
20. Rename the visualization file as "line_chart.png".
21. Copy and paste it to the Desktop.
