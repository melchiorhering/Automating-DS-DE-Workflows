To finish this task, please follow these steps:

1. Click the "Browse Data" button on the left-side panel;
2. Click into Sample Database;
3. Click People.
4. Click Summarize on the top right corner.
5. At "Pick a column to group by", choose "State".
6. Click Done.
7. On the Region Map generated on the left, find the result for number of people who live in Iowa, Montana and Texas, which are 104, 108, 194 respectively.
8. Fill them in the excel file.
