To open the log file of the last run of a task, we can follow the steps:
1. Click "workFlow_Echo" in the Dag column to enter task details page of the dag;
2. In the dag details page, click the rightmost box in the "processing" task row under the histogram on the left side of the UI to enter the corresponding task instance;
3. Click logs in the details row on the right to enter the log page.
